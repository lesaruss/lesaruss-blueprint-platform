## How It Works

Operators enter through a **progressive door system** — each phase collects exactly what it needs, then moves to the next. Nothing is skipped. Every door builds on the one before it.

```
DOOR 1 — Front Door        Big picture intake. Who you are, where you are, what you're building.
DOOR 2 — Deep Dive         Full system audit across brand, offer, content, operations, and gaps.
DOOR 3 — Blueprint Build   Your operating system is built from everything collected.
DOOR 4 — Activation        The system goes live. You start running it.
DOOR 5 — Evolution         New versions are released. You upgrade and keep growing.
```

Every client who moves through the system feeds collective intelligence back into the platform — making the next version stronger for everyone inside it.

## The Blueprint Score

Tier 2 and Tier 3 clients receive a **Blueprint Score** after completing the Door 2 audit — a 100-point assessment across seven categories that determines where their system build starts and what gets prioritized first.

| Category | Points |
|---|---|
| Mission Alignment | 20 |
| Brand Clarity | 18 |
| Offer Strength | 18 |
| Revenue & Traction | 14 |
| Content & Audience | 12 |
| Operations & Systems | 10 |
| Direction & Focus | 8 |

Mission Alignment is the highest-weighted category because LESARUSS only puts its time and name behind brands that are working toward the betterment of people, communities, and the planet. Brands that score below the alignment threshold retain DIY access but are not eligible for Guided, Done For You, or portfolio consideration.

## The 111 Day Theory

The 111 Day Theory is the personal activation system that lives beneath the LESARUSS Blueprint. Before building a brand, a person has to understand the system they're already running — the patterns, beliefs, and defaults that have been shaping their outcomes.

Three phases. 37 days each.

- **SELF** — Know your operating system. Life domain inventory across health, finances, purpose, relationships, mind, and environment.
- **SYSTEM** — Design your blueprint. Build a documented, intentional plan across every domain.
- **ACTIVATE** — Put it into the world. Brand builders enter the LESARUSS system here.

The 111 Day Theory builds the person. The LESARUSS Blueprint builds the brand. Together, they are the full activation path.
