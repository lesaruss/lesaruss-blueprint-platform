# Intake System
*Version 1.0 — Initial draft. Questions will be refined with each system version based on collective client data.*

---

## Overview
The intake system is the engine behind the door model. Every question exists for a reason — to give the system exactly what it needs to build the client's operating system accurately and efficiently. No fluff, no filler questions. Each door collects what it needs, then closes.

The intake is not a form. It is the first experience of the LESARUSS system. The way it is structured — clear, purposeful, progressive — should immediately communicate what kind of platform this is.

---

## Tier Key
Throughout this document, the following shorthand is used to indicate how each section is delivered by tier:

| Symbol | Tier | How It's Administered |
|---|---|---|
| `[ALL]` | All tiers | Delivered the same way to everyone |
| `[DIY]` | Tier 1 | Self-guided — client completes independently |
| `[GDD]` | Tier 2 | Guided — completed with a LESARUSS operator |
| `[DFY]` | Tier 3 | Done For You — LESARUSS team completes via interview + research |

---

## Door 1 — The Front Door
**Trigger:** Client signs up at any tier.
**Administered:** `[ALL]` — same questions for every tier. This is the universal entry point.
**Purpose:** Big picture only. Who are you, where are you, what are you building?

*Instructions to client: Answer each question as honestly and specifically as you can. There are no wrong answers. The more real your answers are, the better the system we build for you.*

---

### Section 1 — Your Brand

**1.1** What is the name of your brand or business?

**1.2** In one sentence, what do you do?
*(Focus on what you do for people, not what you call it.)*

**1.3** Who do you serve? Describe your ideal person in as much detail as you can.
*(Who are they? What do they want? What are they struggling with?)*

**1.4** What makes you different from others doing something similar?
*(If you're not sure yet, that's okay — say that.)*

---

### Section 2 — Your Stage

**2.1** Where are you right now? Select the one that fits best:
- [ ] Idea stage — I haven't launched yet
- [ ] Early — I've started but I'm still figuring it out
- [ ] Established — I have clients/customers and some traction
- [ ] Scaling — I'm growing and need systems to keep up

**2.2** What is your primary goal for the next 90 days?
*(Be specific. "Grow my brand" is not a goal. "Sign 3 new clients" is a goal.)*

**2.3** What have you already tried that hasn't worked the way you hoped?
*(This is one of the most important questions. Be honest.)*

---

### Section 3 — Your Assets

**3.1** Do you have a website? If yes, what is the URL?

**3.2** What social platforms are you currently active on? List them and include your handle.

**3.3** Do you have an existing offer — something you sell or plan to sell? If yes, describe it briefly.

**3.4** Do you have any existing brand assets — logo, colors, fonts? (Yes / No / In progress)

---

### Section 4 — Your Tier

**4.1** Which tier are you entering at?
- [ ] Tier 1 — DIY ($11/month) — I'll run the system myself
- [ ] Tier 2 — Guided ($111/month) — I want support as I build
- [ ] Tier 3 — Done For You ($10,000/quarter) — I want LESARUSS to build and run it for me

**4.2** How did you find LESARUSS?

**4.3** Is there anything else you want us to know before we begin?

---

**Door 1 closes when:** All questions are submitted. Client profile is created. Tier is confirmed. System routes them to Door 2.

---

## Door 2 — The Deep Dive (System Audit)
**Trigger:** Door 1 is complete.
**Administered:** Varies by tier — see tier notes in each section.
**Purpose:** Get specific. Audit the current state of every system: brand, offer, content, operations, and gaps.

*This door goes deeper. Take your time with it. The more specific your answers, the more precisely we can build your system.*

---

### Section 1 — Brand

`[DIY]` Self-guided via this form.
`[GDD]` Completed with a LESARUSS operator in a guided session.
`[DFY]` LESARUSS team conducts a brand research review + client interview.

**1.1** How would you describe your brand in three words?

**1.2** What do you want people to feel when they interact with your brand?
*(Examples: inspired, safe, energized, smart, seen, challenged)*

**1.3** What is the core belief behind your brand? The thing you believe that not everyone agrees with?

**1.4** If your brand were a person, how would you describe their personality?

**1.5** What is your brand's tone of voice?
- [ ] Bold and direct
- [ ] Warm and conversational
- [ ] Educational and informative
- [ ] Inspirational and motivational
- [ ] Professional and authoritative
- [ ] Other: ___________

**1.6** Do you have existing brand guidelines (colors, fonts, logo files)? If yes, please attach or describe them.

---

### Section 2 — Offer

`[DIY]` Self-guided via this form.
`[GDD]` Completed with operator — operator helps clarify and map the offer structure.
`[DFY]` LESARUSS team maps the full offer suite based on interview + market review.

**2.1** List everything you currently sell or plan to sell. Include name, price, and how it's delivered.

**2.2** What is your primary or flagship offer? (The one thing you would keep if you could only keep one.)

**2.3** How does a client get results from your flagship offer? Walk through the process.

**2.4** What does success look like for a client who has gone through your offer?

**2.5** What is your current monthly revenue? (Approximate ranges are fine.)
- [ ] $0 — not yet monetized
- [ ] Under $1,000/month
- [ ] $1,000–$5,000/month
- [ ] $5,000–$10,000/month
- [ ] $10,000–$25,000/month
- [ ] $25,000+/month

**2.6** What is the biggest friction point in your current sales or delivery process?

---

### Section 3 — Content

`[DIY]` Self-guided via this form.
`[GDD]` Completed with operator — operator reviews existing content alongside client responses.
`[DFY]` LESARUSS team audits all active channels and content before the interview.

**3.1** What platforms are you currently creating content on? (List all active ones.)

**3.2** How often are you posting on each platform?

**3.3** What types of content do you create? (Check all that apply)
- [ ] Short-form video (Reels, TikTok, Shorts)
- [ ] Long-form video (YouTube, podcasts)
- [ ] Written posts (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, blogs)
- [ ] Images / graphics / carousels
- [ ] Email newsletters
- [ ] Other: ___________

**3.4** What piece of content has performed best for you? Why do you think it worked?

**3.5** What does your content creation process look like today?
*(Do you batch? Plan ahead? Post spontaneously? Be honest.)*

**3.6** What is your biggest challenge with content right now?

---

### Section 4 — Operations

`[DIY]` Self-guided via this form.
`[GDD]` Completed with operator — operator helps identify tool gaps and workflow issues.
`[DFY]` LESARUSS team conducts a full operations review.

**4.1** What tools are you currently using? List them by category:
- Project/task management: ___________
- Email marketing: ___________
- CRM / client management: ___________
- Content scheduling: ___________
- Website / landing pages: ___________
- Payment processing: ___________
- Other: ___________

**4.2** Do you have a team? If yes, list their roles briefly.

**4.3** What does a typical work week look like for you? How do you spend your time?

**4.4** What tasks take up the most time but feel like they shouldn't?

**4.5** What is currently falling through the cracks in your business?

---

### Section 5 — Gaps and Priorities

`[ALL]` — Same for every tier.

**5.1** On a scale of 1–5, rate how solid each area of your business feels right now:

| Area | Rating (1 = unclear, 5 = dialed in) |
|---|---|
| Brand identity and messaging | |
| Offer and pricing | |
| Content strategy and consistency | |
| Client delivery and experience | |
| Operations and tools | |
| Revenue and growth | |

**5.2** What is the single biggest thing holding your business back right now?

**5.3** If you could fix one thing immediately, what would it be?

**5.4** What does your business look like in 12 months if everything goes right?

---

**Door 2 closes when:** All sections are complete and submitted (or operator session is concluded for GDD, or LESARUSS team interview is complete for DFY). System gap report is generated.

**For Tier 2 and Tier 3 clients:** The Blueprint Score is calculated and delivered before the build begins. See `10_BLUEPRINT_SCORE.md` for the full rubric. The score determines where the blueprint build starts and what gets prioritized first. It also serves as the baseline for re-scoring every 90 days.

**For Tier 1 clients:** No Blueprint Score is issued. The self-rating from Section 5 serves as the client's personal baseline.

Blueprint build begins. Client is routed to Door 3.

---

## Door 3 — Blueprint Approval
**Trigger:** Blueprint build is complete.
**Administered:** `[ALL]` — every tier reviews and approves their blueprint.
**Purpose:** Ensure the system we built accurately reflects the client's brand and goals before activation.

*This is not another form. This is a review and sign-off. The client receives their completed blueprint and confirms it is accurate before we move to activation.*

---

### Blueprint Review Checklist (Client-Facing)

Before approving your blueprint, confirm the following:

- [ ] **Brand Foundation** — Does the mission, vision, and core philosophy feel like you?
- [ ] **Messaging** — Does the core message and voice accurately represent how you want to show up?
- [ ] **Offer Map** — Are all your offers accurately represented with correct pricing and delivery?
- [ ] **Content Plan** — Does the content strategy reflect your platforms, capacity, and goals?
- [ ] **Delivery System** — Does the client journey match how you want to work with people?
- [ ] **Operations** — Are the tools and workflows mapped to how you actually operate?
- [ ] **90-Day Plan** — Does the first 90-day operating plan feel achievable and directionally right?

---

### Door 3 Sign-Off Questions

**3.1** On a scale of 1–10, how accurately does this blueprint represent your brand and business?

**3.2** What, if anything, feels off or needs adjustment?

**3.3** What are you most excited to activate first?

**3.4** Do you approve this blueprint to move to activation? (Yes / No / Needs revision)

---

**Door 3 closes when:** Client approves the blueprint. System moves to Door 4 — Activation.

---

## System Feedback (Post-Door Notes)

After each door closes, the following data is logged to the LESARUSS system for version improvement:

- **Door 1:** Most common "what have you tried" answers — identifies patterns in where operators are coming from
- **Door 2:** Most common gap ratings — identifies where most operators are weakest (informs next version priorities)
- **Door 3:** Average blueprint accuracy score — identifies where builds miss and need refinement
- **Across all doors:** Questions that generate confusion or require clarification — candidates for revision in the next version

This is how every client who moves through the system makes the next version better for everyone.
