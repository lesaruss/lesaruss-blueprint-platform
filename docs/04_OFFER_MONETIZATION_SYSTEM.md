# Offer and Monetization System

## Tiers

### Tier 1
- **Price**: $11/month or $111/year
- **Features**: Basic access to the platform's features, community support, and regular updates.

### Tier 2
- **Price**: $111/month or $1111/year
- **Features**: Advanced features including premium support, additional resources, and exclusive webinars.

### Tier 3
- **Price**: $10000/quarter
- **Features**: All-inclusive access, priority support, personalized consultation, and custom solutions.

- **Variable Costs**: Depending on the specific needs and scope of the project.
