# Offer and Monetization System
*Version 1.0 — Initial draft. This document is a living system and will be updated as the platform evolves.*

---

## Overview
LESARUSS monetizes through access to the operating system. The tier a client enters on determines how much labor LESARUSS performs on their behalf — not how much value they receive. Every tier gets the system. The difference is who does the work.

There are four revenue streams: **Subscription Tiers**, **Done For You Engagements**, **Custom Consultation**, and the **Certification Program**.

---

## Subscription Tiers

### Tier 1 — DIY Operating System
**Price:** $11/month or $111/year
**Who it's for:** The self-starter. Someone who wants access to the LESARUSS system and is willing to do the work themselves. They run their own system, apply their own upgrades, and use the community as their support layer.

**What's included:**
- Full access to the LESARUSS Blueprint Framework (all templates, documentation, and guides)
- Access to all system version updates — client is responsible for applying them
- Community access (peer support, shared learnings, collective system improvement)
- New version release notes with upgrade instructions

**What's not included:**
- Guided sessions or operator support
- Done For You build or management
- Direct access to LESARUSS team

**The logic:** $11 is intentional. It is low enough that cost is not the barrier. The barrier is willingness to do the work. Clients who engage at this tier and see results naturally progress to a higher tier — or they invest in the Certification Program to deepen their skills.

---

### Tier 2 — Guided System
**Price:** $111/month or $1,111/year
**Who it's for:** The operator who wants support. They are willing to do the work but want a LESARUSS operator alongside them — guiding them through the system, helping them apply upgrades, and keeping them from getting stuck.

**What's included:**
- Everything in Tier 1
- Monthly guided sessions with a LESARUSS operator
- Assisted system build (Door 2 and Door 3 completed with operator support)
- Priority upgrade walkthroughs when new versions are released
- Direct feedback on system components as they are built

**What's not included:**
- Full Done For You management
- LESARUSS team running their systems on their behalf

**The logic:** The Guided tier is where most clients graduate to after seeing initial traction at the DIY level. It reduces friction without removing ownership. The client still runs their system — they just don't run it alone.

---

### Tier 3 — Done For You (DIFY)
**Price:** $10,000/quarter
**Who it's for:** The operator who wants results without managing the process. They hand LESARUSS the wheel. LESARUSS builds, activates, manages, and upgrades their entire system. Client reviews, approves, and receives outcomes.

**What's included:**
- Everything in Tier 1 and Tier 2
- Full system build — LESARUSS team completes every door on behalf of the client
- Active system management — LESARUSS runs the operating cadence
- All version upgrades applied by LESARUSS automatically
- Regular performance reviews and system optimization
- Direct access to LESARUSS leadership

**The logic:** At this level, LESARUSS is functioning as the client's operations team. The price reflects the labor, expertise, and accountability we take on. Every DIFY client also becomes a high-value input to the system — their results inform the next version of the platform.

## Custom Consultation
**Starting price:** $2,500
**Final price:** Determined after intake assessment.

For brands or operators that need a custom engagement outside the standard tiers — strategic audits, one-time system builds, org-level implementation, or specialized projects. Consultation clients go through a dedicated intake process and receive a scoped proposal before any work begins.

## Certification Program
**Purpose:** Train external operators in the LESARUSS system. Create a credentialed talent pipeline.

**Who it's for:** Individuals who want to master the LESARUSS operating system — either to apply it to their own brand at a deeper level, or to become a certified LESARUSS operator and work with clients inside the program.

**How it works:**
1. Applicant applies for the Certification Program
2. They pay the certification tuition (pricing TBD — to be defined in a future version)
3. They go through a structured training curriculum covering every system component
4. Upon passing the assessment, they receive LESARUSS Operator Certification
5. Certified operators enter the LESARUSS talent pool and are eligible for placement as the platform grows

**The logic:** As LESARUSS scales, it needs operators to run Guided and Done For You engagements. Rather than hiring externally, we grow our own talent — from within the community. People who believe in the system enough to pay to learn it are exactly the people we want representing it. They invest in themselves to create an opportunity. That is aligned incentive.

## Upgrade Path
The natural progression through the LESARUSS system creates an organic upgrade path:

**DIY → Guided:** Client sees results, wants support to go faster or go deeper.
**Guided → DIFY:** Client has proven the system works and wants LESARUSS to run it for them.
**Any tier → Certification:** Client wants to master the system and create an opportunity from their expertise.

Every tier is designed to create pull toward the next level — not through pressure, but through results.
